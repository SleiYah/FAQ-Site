How do I reset my password?
To reset your password, click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You will receive an email with instructions to create a new password. Follow the link in the email and enter your new password when prompted.
Can I change my username?
Currently, usernames cannot be changed after registration. If you need to use a different username, you would need to create a new account. However, you can update your display name in your profile settings.
How do I delete my account?
To delete your account, go to your account settings and scroll to the bottom of the page. Click on "Delete Account" and follow the confirmation steps. Please note that this action is permanent and all your data will be permanently removed from our servers.
Is my data secure?
Yes, we take data security very seriously. All personal information is encrypted and stored securely. We do not share your information with third parties without your explicit consent. For more details, please refer to our Privacy Policy.
How can I contact customer support?
You can contact our customer support team by sending an email to support@example.com. Our team is available Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM EST. We typically respond to inquiries within 24 business hours.
